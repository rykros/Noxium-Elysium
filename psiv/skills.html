Skills in Phantasy Star IV are powerful abilities that characters can use during battle to gain a tactical advantage. Unlike techniques, which are akin to magic spells and shared among multiple characters, skills are unique to each character, emphasizing their individual strengths and roles within the party. For example, a character like Chaz might have access to potent physical skills, while Rune focuses on devastating magical attacks, and Raja uses healing and support abilities. This uniqueness helps to define each character's combat style and strategy.

The skill point system in Phantasy Star IV determines how often a character can use their skills. Each skill has a limited number of uses, represented by skill points that deplete with each activation. Unlike techniques that consume TP, skills are not tied to a shared resource and can only be replenished by resting at an inn or using specific items. Once a character's skill points are exhausted, they cannot use that skill again until their points are restored.

Managing skill points is crucial for long battles or dungeon explorations, as it prevents characters from over-relying on their most powerful moves. Knowing when to unleash these abilities and when to conserve them adds an extra layer of strategy to combat, ensuring that each encounter feels dynamic and challenging.
