WELCOME!

Hello! My name is Ricky "Rykros" Runyon, and welcome to Noxium Elysium, my personal webspace. This site is a personal project where I'll be showcasing my work as a full-stack engineer and hosting all of my other projects. One of my featured pages is dedicated to the Sega Genesis RPG, Phantasy Star IV: The End of the Millennium—a game very dear to my heart and my personal favorite. There are currently bios on all of the main characters in the game. I plan to expand on that content over time, so stay tuned! I've also written a few blog posts that you might find interesting, and I'll be adding more regularly. If you'd like to learn more about me and my journey in software engineering, check out my About Me page. Right now, it serves as my portfolio, though this project is currently the only one listed. I'm working on expanding it to better reflect my growing skills. Feel free to explore the site, and if you have any questions, ideas, or just want to chat, shoot me an email! You can also find links to my GitHub and social media accounts in the footer. Thanks for visiting!
