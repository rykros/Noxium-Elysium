ZIO

PHANTASY STAR COMPENDIUM BIO

A black magician who worships Dark Force. He has founded an evil cult and made Nurvus run amok to cause destruction. He wears a gauntlet on only one arm to hide the fact that his right arm has been monster-fied. He is the opponent in the first half of the tale.
