RUNE WALSH

(Thray Walsh)

AMERICAN INSTRUCTION MANUAL BIO

CLASS: Wizard
AGE: ???
Rune is an old friend of Alys, though how they first met remains a mystery. How Alys managed to put up with him is a bigger mystery still. He's powerful and arrogant, and wields a type of power rarely seen on Motavia anymore: true magic.

PHANTASY STAR COMPENDIUM BIO

A master magician who, of course, can use techniques, but who also wields magic thought to have died out long ago. He's an obnoxious man who constantly treats Rudy (Chaz) like a child, but, in truth, he is the inheritor of the will and memory of Lutz, the one who fought with Alisa 2,000 years ago.
