Alys's prowess as a Hunter has made her famous throughout Motavia. Not much is known about her past, but bits of it seem to keep poping up at odd moments... much to her embarrassment. She is most proficient with thrown weapons such as bommerangs and slicers.

PHANTASY STAR COMPENDIUM BIO

Rudy's (Chaz's) teacher, a master Hunter called "Tear 'Em to Pieces Lyla." Three years ago she met Rudy, took him in, and brought out his true character. During the battle with Zio, she is hit with the Black Wave while protecting Rudy and loses her life.
